Terms and Conditions for “3-Star Integrity Quest: Accuracy Confirmation” Campaign (Round 1)

2. Eligibility

  • Participants must be subscribers of the Precious Coin Tester app.

  • Employees of the Organizer and their immediate family members are not eligible to participate.

3. Campaign Period

The Campaign begins on January 1, 2024, and ends on June 30, 2024, or when a false positive is confirmed and rewarded, whichever comes first.

4. Participation Process

  • Participants must use the latest version of the Precious Coin Tester app (version 1.4.3 or higher) for testing coins.
  • If a Participant suspects a 3-star result is incorrect, they must follow the reporting and verification procedures as outlined in the Campaign communication.

5. Rewards

  • A $30 (USD) or equivalent reward will be given for the first confirmed false positive.
  • The reward will be paid minus any applicable bank transfer fees.
  • The decision of the Organizer regarding reward recipients is final and binding.

6. Destructive Analysis Consent

  • To reveal the exact precious metal content, a destructive analysis using potentiometric titration or an equivalent method will be conducted by a third party.

  • By physically sending a coin to the Organizer for analysis, Participants irrevocably consent to the destruction of the coin.
